I am a Ph.D. student in the School of Computer Science and Engineering at Beihang University. Before that, I received my M.S. degree in Artificial Intelligence from Beijing Normal University in 2024 and my B.S. degree in Computer Science from South China Normal University in 2021.

My research interests include computer vision, image processing, multimodal learning, and personalized vision-language models. I am particularly interested in building unified models for personalized visual understanding and generation.

Previously, I have worked on low-light image enhancement, zero-shot night-time image captioning, video summarization, and multimodal educational agents.

I am currently exploring personalized vision-language models, especially how to enable models to understand and generate visual content involving personalized concepts.
